About Save Our Sound

Our Mission

Save Our Sound was founded in 2020 with a simple but urgent mission: to preserve Trinidad and Tobago's rich musical heritage through the collection, restoration, and preservation of vintage vinyl records.

As streaming services dominate the music landscape, the unique character and cultural significance of physical media risk being lost forever.

Why Trinidad's Music Matters

Cultural Heritage

From calypso's social commentary to soca's carnival energy, our music tells the story of our nation.

Historical Significance

Early recordings capture performances and styles that would otherwise be lost to time.

Artistic Value

Album artwork and liner notes provide context and visual art that digital formats lack.
