Our Services

Comprehensive vinyl record services for collectors and enthusiasts

Record Buyback Program

We purchase vintage Trinidadian vinyl records in good condition. Our experts will evaluate your records and offer fair market prices.

  • Calypso records from the 1950s-1980s
  • Soca and steelpan recordings
  • Local gospel and folk music
  • Rare pressings and limited editions

Restoration Services

Bring your damaged records back to life with our professional restoration services:

  • Deep cleaning and anti-static treatment
  • Minor scratch repair
  • Jacket and sleeve restoration
  • Digital transfer services
